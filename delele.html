Account Deletion Policy for Stuti

Effective Date: 19 Jun 2026

Requesting Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Stuti account and associated personal information at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request account deletion, please send an email from your registered email address to the support team with:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Your full name.
  • A request stating that you wish to delete your Stuti account.

Information Deleted

Upon successful verification and processing of the request, the following information may be deleted:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Account profile information
  • Associated application access records

Information Retained

Certain records may be retained where required by applicable laws, legal obligations, security requirements, or dispute resolution purposes.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of successful verification.
