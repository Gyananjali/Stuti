Introduction

Stuti is a devotional audio and music streaming application developed by Gyananjali Prachyavidya Sansthanam. The application provides access to spiritual discourses, class recordings, stutis, mantras, shlokas, and other devotional audio content exclusively for authorized Gyananjali members.

1. Information We Collect

To provide access to member-only content, the application may collect and store:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Basic account identification information required for authentication

No financial information is collected through the application.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide access to authorized member content.
  • To manage user accounts.
  • To improve application functionality and user experience.
  • To communicate important service-related information.

All audio recordings, class recordings, stutis, shlokas, lectures, and devotional content available through the application are owned by Gyananjali Prachyavidya Sansthanam and are protected by applicable copyright laws.

4. Android Auto

The application supports Android Auto to enable safe playback of devotional audio content while driving. No additional personal information is collected through Android Auto functionality.

5. Data Sharing

6. Data Security

7. Children's Privacy
